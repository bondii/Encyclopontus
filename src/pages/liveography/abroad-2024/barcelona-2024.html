I did expect to like Barcelona, but not this much, honestly...

Street feeling

I walk through the streets and I think:

What a cute little street, with cute little shops and elegant architecture!

There's a mix of old and young people on the streets, talking, throwing balls, taking pictures, lauging, sitting by themselves, enjoying the sun. It almost feels like I'm in some kind of game world or movie set, just that it's not as "perfect" as those memories...

I must have gotten straight into one of the best parts of the city!

...but each street is similarly interesting, with its own variations. Here and there's some obviously touristy souvenir shop, and here and there's some really nice or useful boutique, like a fruit shop, or a tight asian store.

I keep walking and the streets keep changing with new things to see and I realize, "this" is not the "best part" of the Barcelona, this is Barcelona!

I'm probably just romanticizing some kind of honeymoon phase feeling

but I do know I have never had such a nice feeling the instant I was walking in a city, which then endured for (so far) the whole stay!
