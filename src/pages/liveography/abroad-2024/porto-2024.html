Porto ~ 2024-02-13 -> 2024-03-01

I have been to Portugal four times before, but only for going to festivals and not to enjoy the country itself. Sure, we've stayed in Airbnb's out on the countryside just 100% relaxing and recovering after a week of partying, but this has only left me with a taste for actually experiencing the country, not any real feel for how it is.

Hence when my sister got the keys to her apartment in Porto and invited me to come stay with her, it was an obvious yes.

Porto is a really cozy city, with very varied city parts and views. Amazing waves both for watching and surfing. Iconic bridges over the beautiful wide river, with cute houses all over the steep slope down to the water. Elegant luxurious small houses right next to battered abandoned ones. A countryside with jungle-isch vibes (in the eyes of a northerner) and picturesque stone villages which leaves me both wondering how people even live there as well as want to try it out myself.

All in all I had a great time, finding some quiet for relaxation and soft exploration of the city, as well as good focus for my work.
Well needed after the month in Barcelona!
