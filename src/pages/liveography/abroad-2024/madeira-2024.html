Madeira ~ 2024-03-01 -> 2024-04-02

Madeira is so far my favorite semester location of all time. Starting with the most necessary caveat: The island is very touristy and highly exploited. You will not feel like you're in proper wilderness, compared to e.g. the Swedish mountains. That being said, as a semester location I personally don't mind this, and rather find the high level of tourism makes it more comfortable and easy to e.g. rent a scooter and find information about where to find trekking routes etc. In fact, this accessibility is what makes me consider it my favorite semester location, as the stay was mostly pure enjoyment and free from hassles.
