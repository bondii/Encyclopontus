Social Relaxation

When a group socializes while drinking alcohol, I've often noticed the topics of discussion going haywire without anyone seeming to care. Obviously this is not the case all the time, but often enough to make me write this text. It's like the value of the discussion doesn't lie in what is being discussed, but rather just that we as a group are discussing and sharing thoughts for a while, filling up our Social Need bar.

Assuming this to be true, I want to argue that "talking bullshit" in this sense is kind of equivalent to a social meditation, where one can save on precious Energy Spoons (not delving deep into the intricacies of subjects often means we don't really have to do anything else than Level 1 or maybe Level 2 thinking), while we still get our social needs met, leaving us with a more balanced and content feeling when we return on our solo paths afterwards.
